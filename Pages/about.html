Key Features

  • Login System: Users can securely log in with their username and password.
  • Menu Navigation: After a successful login, users can navigate to different pages within the app.
  • Genre Selection: Users can choose a music genre from a drop-down menu to see a list of pre-selected songs.
  • Multiple Genre Selection: The "Select More Genre" button allows users to explore additional song lists without having to log out.
  • Comment Section: Users can leave their opinions and feedback about the app experience directly on the genre pages.
  • Logoff Button: Provides a convenient way for users to log out and return to the login page securely.

How It Works

After logging in, users land on a main page with options to access the menu or log off. Selecting the menu button takes users to a genre selection page where they can choose from various music genres. Once a genre is selected, a curated list of songs for that genre is displayed. Users can either explore more genres or provide feedback using the comment section. The logoff option remains available at all stages for easy navigation back to the login page.

Why You'll Love It

Our Music Application is built to offer a simple and streamlined way to access music genres while incorporating user feedback to continually improve the experience. Whether you're a music enthusiast or simply looking to discover new tunes, this app makes it easy to explore and engage.
